About

I'm a second-year PhD student in the Linguistics department at Northwestern University. My broad focus is computational sociolinguistics and I have interests in natural language processing, social meaning and identity construction, and data science for social good. I'm advised by Rob Voigt and part of the Linguistic Mechanisms lab.
